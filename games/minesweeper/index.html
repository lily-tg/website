How to play

The objective of the game is to click every tile that doesn't have a mine.

When you click on the tiles, you may notice some have numbers in them. These numbers tell you how many tiles around it have mines in them.

There is a flag button. Once you click it, you can flag tiles to note there are mines there. Press it again to return to clicking the tiles.


To play again, restart the page. Mine generation is random
