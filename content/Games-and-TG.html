Transcript

Stuart was an avid gamer, but recently, he coudln't find more games to play, to the point where he was constantly bored. One day, one of his friends sent him a link for an interesting experience. At first, it looked like an average RPG, but each time his character leveled up, his body changed a little.

First, his hair grew to the waist. Then his skin became slender and softer. Meanwhile, his face got rounder. His clothes transformed into a delicate white dress. And at last, his genitals inverted. The new girl was too inmersed in the game to notice anything, so she continued playing until she heard a voice from the kitchen.

"Stacy, it's time for dinner!"

"I'm coming, mom!"
