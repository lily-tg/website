A school restart

Requested by jaymiejordan.

Transcript

Nick was a recent collage graduate who had trouble searching for a new job, since his career wasn't very appreciated in society. One day, he was walking through the streets when he saw a wishing well. He didn't believe that much in them, but it didn't hurt to try.

He wished to go through school again, so he could choose a different career, and then slipped a coin inside. At first, nothing seemed to happen, but as he started to walk back home, everything started to change.

First, he started to get shorter. Then, his chest expanded, his skin softened and his genitals inverted. His clothes changed into a school uniform with a skirt in it. The wind blew, showing his, or rather, her now long hair.

The girl stopped walking and started to notice the changes. The name 'Nikki' came to mind as she conviniently found her school supplies at home. She would have to make a good first impression this year.
