Office Woman TG

Transcript

There was a man searching for a job in a hopeless economy. After being fired from his previous job, he was feeling in the dumps and very unproductive. He checked plenty of posts, but none worked out. Being pretty dissapointed in himself, he decided to go outside.

While walking around, he found a cofee shop, and inside, the barista recomended some special coffee that she said should help him get back on track. So, he bought the package and brought it back home. Since he didn't have a coffee machine, he bought one too.

The next day, he started preparing the coffee he had gotten previously with his also recently adquired coffee machine. It tasted wierd at first, but he started getting used to it. He could feel himself getting used to it.

Unbeknownst to him, his body started changing around, along with his surroundings. After he drank her fifth cup, she looked around and saw herself in an office environment. Her job might be a little repetitive, but at least it pays decently.
