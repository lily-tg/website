Transcript

Katubbus was having a decently normal day, enjoying her peaceful life in the world. Today was a relaxing day, where she could finally have a break to unwind herself. However, she drank a lot of water earlier and felt an urge to go to the bathroom to relieve herself.

Once inside, after doing her necessities, she observed the mirror and noticed a cigarette that wasn't there before. 'How unusual', she thought. Out of curiosity, she takes it. Before she could do anything, it started to glow ominously.

First, her fur started slowly changing color from light green to a more reddish orange. Her clothes morphed themselves into a white robe and a black skirt. She wouldn't notice, but the environment was being affected by the transformation.

She opened a closet and found some golden chains, a few earrings and a classy red jacket. As thoughts about high class life started entering her mind, her mobile phone became a pack of cigarettes, the trans flag decorating her room was now a snapshot of the stock market. Everything started looking more like a rich CEO's room. Even her bed became more high end.

Before going outside to check her company's stock, she decided to look at herself in the bathroom mirror. Maybe she would smoke a little more. After all, she didn't need to go to the office just now, unlike the peasants who worked for her. She no longer remembered who she was before.
