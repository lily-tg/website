Love at first TG

Transcript

Albert was in love with one of his classmates at school, named Madison. She was cute, smart and her hair flowed nicely. Unfortunately, he was never able to start a relationship with her as he wasn't her type. One day, he wished on a shooting star to be attractive enough for his crush.

When he woke up, he noticed two orbs hanging on his chest. After examining the room and checking a mirror, Albert realized he had turned into a girl.

Albertine opened her closet and picked a cute outfit. At school, she was getting used to her new situation. After classes, she went to the park and found a familiar classmate.

Madison turned around and saw Albertine walking towards her. After buying some food, they started talking about life, fashion and sweets. A few months later, Albertine asked her crush out, at least for Valentine's day.

"Madison, you're so cute. Would you like to be my Valentine's?"

"Of course, my dear Albertine."
