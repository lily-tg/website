John to Emma TG AP

Transcript

John Yokashi had recently adquired Pokemon Legends Z-A and started playing it. As he advanced through the game, he started meeting new characters. And so, he eventually found Emma, a young detective from Luminose City who looks really pretty.

One day, while walking back from school, he comes across a mysterious Espurr plush and decides to bring it home. He names it “Mimi” after Emma's closest companion. While he continues playing the game, he starts imagining how cool it would be to be the great detective from town. Unconciously making a wish, causing the plush to activate and suddenly, sucking him into the Switch with it.

John's body starts changing slowly. He starts growing taller, his hair curls down into a short bob. His clothes morph into a suit with a Key Stone and a sweather with buttons. Now they can megaevolve a pokemon in battle. Their hips get wider and their rear grows little by little. The plush starts gaining sentience and becomes a real Espurr.

John, now a very tall girl with a very curvy figure, wakes up in a very familiar place, but she can't fully pin down where she is. Once she explores the area and gets access to a mirror, she realizes she has fully become Emma.

“How did I even get here” Emma wonders to herself
“Espurr, Espurr” Her companion says.
Emma can't bring herself to wonder too much and decides to go outside to help as many people as she can in Luminose City.
