Big Dolly Sister

Transcript

Halloween was fast approaching and two siblings were arguing about their costumes. The younger sister, Molly, was a big fan of The Amazing Digital Circus, so she decided to dress as Pomni. The older brother, Matt, didn't have a plan for the holiday, but his sister was really insisting about him getting a costume.

At night, Molly went out to explore the town and found a stiched dress with a red wig and a big bow. She urged Matt to try it on until he gave in. However, when he finally got dressed, something strange started to happen.

Matt's skin started to take on a ragged texture. He tried taking off the wig, but it wouldn't budge anymore. He started growing rapidly, both in height and in body proportions. Once he, or rather she, was starting to process her changes, a big button started to grow on her right eye, but surprisingly, it didn't feel painful.

Once the changes were done, Molly stared at her, amazed at the sudden turn of events. She decided to hug her now big sister who now looked like Ragatha. After some discussion, they went treat or treating together and proceed to get a lot of candy. It helped that Matt liked her new body, since she kinda liked the character a lot.
