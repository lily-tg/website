The white hoodie

Transcript

Luke used to be a lonely boy who hated his looks and how everyone else expected him to act.

One day, he found a white hoodie in the middle of the streets. Unbeknownst to him, it was cursed, and so he watched in confusion and excitement as his body changed into one of a cute girl.

He became surprised at her new voice and appareance. She's now Lucy, a girl who loves her looks and her new white hoodie.
