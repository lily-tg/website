Blowing a new life

Transcript

Luis had been bored with life for quite some time. One day, while watching TV, he recieved an unknown package. Upon further inspection, it was a bubblegum box. Inside was written the phrase: "Blow your problems into a new life".

Since he had nothing better to do, he started chewing the gum. After a while, he was ready to make a bubble. As the bubble got bigger, his skin got smoother, his hips went wider and his hair grew longer. However, right after his privates inverted themselves, the bubble blew out.

At this point, Lucia had an idea. She checked the closet and found a guitar she hadn't used in a while. She tied her hair into a ponytail and got new clothes for herself. She brought her instrument to a studio and got ready to practice.

Deciding she liked the gum's flavor, she took more of it with her and started blowing another bubble. Maybe one day she could form a band, but first, she needed to improve her skills.
