A personal transition

Transcript

Mike had secretly wanted to be a girl for a long time. Because of this, he didn't talk very much and had trouble with social interactions. Eventually he told his friend Clara about this, but she didn't know how to help him.

Together, they searched for informations about what it means to feel like the opposite gender and learned more about trans people. After some therapy sessions, Mike was finally ready to start transition.

First, he changed his name to 'Michelle', then she started taking hormones for about a year, and a last, she got bottom surgery. It has been 3 years since then.

Clara noticed how her friend was herself when she took a selfie to remember the moment. Michelle had developed quite a large set of boobs, and she couldn't be happier.
