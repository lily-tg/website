The ramen buffet

Suggested by TheyWhoAbide.

Transcript

Kevin and Stuart were two boys who couldn't manage to get a girl for Valentine's day. After school, they started walking down the street when they saw a ramen shop. Today there was an all-you-can-eat buffet special for single girls because of the holiday. Unfortunately, there wasn't a similar offer for boys.

Stuart suddenly had a brilliant idea. Using some sex-change pills he had adquiered in the dark web (don't ask why he had them) they could become girls for a day and enjoy a lot of ramen from the limited time offer. After thinking of new names and buying some clothes, they were finally ready to eat.

When they arrived, they asked to participate in the buffet and were easily able to get their hands on the food. Stacy was happily enjoying the ramen when she said: "See, Kelly? This was a great idea. We won't have to pay that much for the food." Kelly just continued eating, but then she thought: "The ramen was really cheap before. This was totally unnecesary."
