Fresh Tomato Day TG

Transcript

It was April 6, and this day is considered “Fresh Tomato Day”. It's generally celebrated by eating fresh tomatoes, be it by themselves, on a tomato sandwhich, or on any variation of food one might think of, and ocassionally throwing some at other people, because why not?

Jake was enjoying the festivity in a peaceful way. After going to a store to buy fresh tomatoes, he went to a park to eat them. Some people were making chaos, but he paid them no mind. He started eating calmly, when suddenly one thrown tomato struck him in the head.

For some strange reason, the tomato was slowly making his hair longer. As more tomatoes were thrown at him, whether intentionally or not, his skin started getting smoother. At one point, he even started growing boobs. Eventually, she had fully become a girl, to the point even her clothes got affected in some way or another.

Jane continued eating tomatoes like nothing had happened. When she finished eating all of them, she returned to the store to buy more. Once inside, someone noticed she had a tomato inside her hair and mentioned it to her. So, she checked a mirror and removed it.

Now that she thinks about it, she kinda looks cute.
