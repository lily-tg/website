A cute quiz experience

Suggested by sussybaka420man.

Transcript

Kevin and Steve are friends who enjoy playing viedogames and sharing online content to each other. One day, while cheking new content on the internet, they find a personality quiz titled: "Which Splatoon character would you be?".

It went like a standard internet quiz until they reached the end. Suddenly, it asked one last question: "Did you like the result?". They clicked 'yes', thinking it was a way to rate the quiz, not knowing they would transform into the characters they got.

The transformation happened too fast to react, but they could look at themselves in the mirror. Kevin looked like Agent 3, with her greeny tentacles resembling long hair, while Steve took the appearance of Agent 8, with a curly style in a pinkish color.

They started holding hands, with Agent 3 slightly blushing because of this. Though she would have to admit that they kinda look cute togheter. Meanwhile, Agent 8 was examining curiously at her good friend's expressions.
